Solar Powered Backpack Unit

visuals

Objective:

Create a modular solar powered unit that can be implanted into almost all types of bags/backpacks.

Technical Details

With a time limit of two weeks and a budget of $50, the team came up with an idea to effectively provide mobile solar power for an affordable price. THe total unit costs around $20 and takes less than 5 minutes to assemble.

Results/Outcomes

Overall, the prototype was a success. The solar powered unit was able to passively charge a powerbank to full capacity which in turn charged multiple phones. The main challenge the team faced was the time restraint.

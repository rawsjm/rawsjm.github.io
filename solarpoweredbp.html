Solar Powered Backpack Unit

Goals:

The Solar Powered Backpack Unit was designed for the University of Oklahoma Freshman Engineering Experience competition. The goal was to decide on a project that shows the engineering design process and provide proper documentation. My group decided to use this idea becuase it fell within the $50 budget and could be completed within the two-week timeframe. At the competition, our team won runner-up with our only demerit being the absence of the rest of the team.

The goal of the project was to create a mobile source of energy to charge mobile devices on the go. The unit would allow the user to attatch it to virtually any backpack for less than $20.

What I learned

I learned how to create and use a Gantt Chart and a selection matrix. I also taught myself to solder, use the Inventor CAD program, and efficiently manage a team of people.

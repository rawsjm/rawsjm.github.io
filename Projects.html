Projectile Motion Study home flight soap solar_power sports_baseball draw hexagon

Goal

Use the kinematics equations along with dimensional analysis to create a spreadsheet that determines whether a ball hit at a specified angle and velocity will go over the "Green Monster" at the Fenway Ballpark. Additionally, create another sheet that determines the minimum velocity needed at a specified angle to achieve a home run. This has also been implemented into a MATLAB application to provide easy and instant inputs/outputs.

Things I've learned

  • Advanced Microsoft Excel Functions
  • Dimensional Analysis
  • MATLAB App development
